Our Roots

SOAR (Student Organized Academic Refinement) is a collective of volunteer educators and students with a mission of making educational opportunities free and accessible through peer-led efforts. Founded in November 2020, we originally aimed to resolve the general drop in academics caused by the pandemic but we have grown into so much more. We are most known for our ongoing efforts to create open peer-led review sessions in order to reinforce knowledge taught in the classroom. However, we also have an incredible and diverse community of students who are making a difference in their schools. We welcome anyone regardless of affiliation, background, or status.

Our Vision

Accessible knowledge for everyone without restrictions.

Our Mission

To provide students with resources and tools to utilize knowledge, and the opportunity to interact with the community through guided programs to better prepare for examinations.
